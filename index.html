Hello, I am Ruth Kupolati, a User experience researcher. I specialize in usability testing, heuristic evaluation, wireframing, user interviews and enjoy solving user problems. My skills include Miro, Figma, Invision, Sketch, Google tools, design thinking methodology and user research

I am a graduate of communications in technology with 3 years of customer and client relationship building in diverse industries. I enjoy creating excellent and memorable user experience for individuals. I am a strong willed, adaptable and intuitive individual. I enjoy fishing out the ‘WHY’ of a problem and ‘HOW’ to go about solving the problem. I call myself a doer! My previous roles have been in the non profit, media advertising, start-ups and health tech industries here in Toronto, Ontario Canada, but am open to remote roles in the United States and within Canada.
